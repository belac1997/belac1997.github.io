The Good Boy Of The Week

This weeks G.B.O.T.W. goes to Otto from Jacksonville Florida. Otto is a mutt and loves going for walks. After each walk, all he wants to do is jump into the pool to cool off, but he does not swim, he just hangs out on the steps. Otto's favorite game is fetch and he always returns the stick back to the thrower and waits for the stick to be thrown again.

Otto's Favorite Activities
Walking The Neighborhood
Loves meeting new dogs
Playing Fetch
Likes the largest sticks
Wading in the pool
Stays on the pool steps
Car Rides
Takes the driver seat
